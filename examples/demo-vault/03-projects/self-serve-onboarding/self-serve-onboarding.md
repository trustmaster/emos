## Objective

Let new merchants onboard and take their first payment without a support handoff.

## Problem statement

Onboarding currently requires a manual review step that adds 2–3 days and a
support ticket per merchant. Self-serve would cut time-to-first-payment and
support load.

## Approach / Solutions

Guided setup flow + automated verification, with a manual fallback for edge cases.

## Risks & Blockers

| Risk / Blocker | Impact | Mitigation | Owner |
|----------------|--------|------------|-------|
| Fraud checks need manual review | Medium | Risk-scored auto-approve with fallback | marco |
