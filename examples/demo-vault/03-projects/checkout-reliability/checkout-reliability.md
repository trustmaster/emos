## Objective

Cut checkout error rate and P99 latency to within SLA and keep them there, after a
quarter of recurring latency incidents.

## Problem statement

Checkout P99 latency has breached its 800ms SLA in three of the last six weeks,
peaking during a June 20 incident at 4.2s. Root causes cluster around unbounded
payment-provider retries and missing backpressure. Error rate during spikes hit
2.1% (SLA: 0.5%).

## Approach / Solutions

1. **Idempotency keys** on payment retries so retries are safe and bounded.
2. **Backpressure + timeouts** on the provider client.
3. **Observability**: a latency/error dashboard and alerting before customers notice.

## Risks & Blockers

| Risk / Blocker | Impact | Mitigation | Owner |
|----------------|--------|------------|-------|
| Provider rate limits under retry storms | High | Idempotency + capped retries | sam |
| Dashboard lag hides slow burns | Medium | 1-min resolution alerting | dana |
