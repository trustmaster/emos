## Research goal & questions

Understand *why* merchants abandon onboarding before first payment, to prioritize the
self-serve MVP requirements.

- Q1: At which step do merchants stall, and what are they waiting on?
- Q2: Does the manual review delay cause abandonment, or is it the setup effort itself?
- Q3: What would make merchants confident enough to finish in one sitting?

## Method

Recruit 8 merchants who started onboarding in the last 30 days but did not reach first
payment. 30-min semi-structured interviews walking through their actual setup attempt. Link
the protocol here.
